How to Play Speed Stars

Game Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Control vehicle direction
  • Space Bar: Activate boost
  • Shift: Drift
  • C: Change camera view

Basic Tips

  • Master the drift mechanics for tight corners
  • Use boost strategically on straightaways
  • Learn each track's shortcuts
  • Customize your vehicle based on track conditions
